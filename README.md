## 3. Project Title

**Online Stationery Store Management System**

---

## 4. Problem Statement

* Many stationery stores still use manual sales and inventory tracking.
* Customers must physically visit the store to check product availability.
* There is no real-time stock monitoring system.
* Managers cannot easily generate sales or inventory reports.
* Manual record keeping increases errors and time consumption.

Therefore, an online system is needed to automate sales, inventory management, and reporting processes.

---

## 5. Project Objectives

* To design a web-based platform for selling stationery products online.
* To automate inventory management.
* To allow customers to browse and purchase products easily.
* To enable staff to manage orders and stock efficiently.
* To generate sales and inventory reports for managers.

---

## 6. Scope of the System

### Included:

* User registration and login
* Product browsing and search
* Shopping cart
* Order placement
* Online payment (simulation)
* Inventory management
* Order management
* Sales reporting

### Not Included:

* Mobile application
* Physical delivery management system
* Integration with external accounting software
